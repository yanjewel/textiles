Application Requirements Affect the Specification

A bag fabric and a cover fabric may need different coating, hand feel, thickness, color fastness and packing. Please share the final application so the quotation can be discussed around practical production and finishing details.

How to Send a Clear Oxford Inquiry

Please send denier or construction reference, target width, coating requirement, color, quantity, roll length, packing method, FOB port or CIF destination port. Photos of the intended finished product can help confirm the fabric direction.
