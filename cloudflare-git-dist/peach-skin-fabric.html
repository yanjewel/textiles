Why Specification Samples Matter

Peach skin fabric can vary by hand feel, brushing level, color shade and finishing route. Photos are useful, but a physical reference sample or clear technical sheet helps both sides confirm the target more accurately.

How to Send a Clear Peach Skin Inquiry

Please include width, weight, color, surface feel, printing or solid-dyed requirement, quantity, packing method and destination port. If the fabric will be used for bedding or apparel, share the intended product so finishing can be discussed properly.

Sampling, MOQ and Hand-Feel Approval

For peach skin fabric, hand feel and surface touch are key approval points. MOQ, sampling time and bulk lead time depend on color, printing, finishing and order quantity. Buyers are encouraged to confirm reference sample, lab dip or printed strike-off before bulk production.

Packing and Inspection Notes

Roll length, packing method, shipping marks and label requirements should be confirmed in writing. Buyers can request packing photos or pre-shipment photos for order follow-up, especially when the fabric will be used for bedding, garment or decorative textile programs.
