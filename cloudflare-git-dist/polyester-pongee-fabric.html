Typical Applications

Pongee is often selected when buyers need a light woven polyester base with practical handling and flexible finishing options. Please confirm end use early because lining, outdoor wear, bag fabric and cover fabric may require different finish routes.

How to Send a Clear Pongee Inquiry

A clear inquiry should include density or reference sample, width, weight, color, finish, order quantity, packing method, shipment term and destination port. Photos of the target fabric help the factory review the surface and hand feel faster.

Sampling, MOQ and Color Confirmation

Pongee orders are usually discussed from a target specification or a physical reference sample. MOQ, sampling time and bulk lead time should be confirmed according to the color, finish, printing or coating requirement. For custom color orders, buyers should confirm lab dip or color standard before bulk production.

Packing and Pre-Shipment Checkpoints

Before shipment, buyers can confirm roll length, roll packing, shipping marks, label format and carton or bale requirements. For FOB or CIF orders, packing photos, roll marks and export document requirements can be discussed before goods leave the factory.
