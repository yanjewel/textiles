Hand Feel Is the Key Detail

Brushed fabric sourcing should not rely on product name alone. The final result depends on base fabric, brushing route, weight, finishing and hand feel target. Reference samples help reduce repeated adjustment during sampling.

How to Send a Clear Brushed Fabric Inquiry

Please share the target hand feel, width, weight, color, application, quantity, packing method and shipment term. If the buyer has a sample, close-up photos and usage details are helpful before a formal quotation.

Sampling, MOQ and Surface Approval

Brushed fabric should be approved by surface effect and hand feel, not only by fabric name. MOQ, sample timing and bulk lead time depend on base fabric, brushing level, color and finishing route. Reference samples or close-up photos are useful for faster matching.

Packing and Quality Follow-Up

Buyers can confirm roll packing, roll length, labels, shipping marks and inspection focus before bulk shipment. For warm-touch home textile or garment programs, shade, surface consistency and packing condition should be checked before loading.
